About |Greenwood High School

Founded in 1985, |Greenwood High School has been at the forefront of educational excellence, nurturing young minds to become responsible global citizens.

Our Mission & Vision

We are committed to providing a nurturing environment where students can discover their passions, develop critical thinking skills, and build strong character foundations.

Personalized learning approach for each student
State-of-the-art technology integration
Comprehensive extracurricular programs
Strong community partnerships

Academic Programs

Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire students at every level.

Elementary School

Building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and social skills through engaging, hands-on learning experiences.

Middle School

Developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills through interdisciplinary projects and exploratory learning.

High School

College-preparatory curriculum with Advanced Placement courses and specialized career pathways.

Our Facilities

Modern learning environments designed to inspire creativity and collaboration.

Science Labs

Fully equipped laboratories for physics, chemistry, and biology experiments.

Library & Media Center

Extensive collection of books, digital resources, and collaborative study spaces.

Athletic Complex

State-of-the-art gymnasium, swimming pool, and outdoor sports facilities.
