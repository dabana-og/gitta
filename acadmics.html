School Overview

Greenwood High School has been nurturing young minds since 1990. Our mission is to provide a holistic education that balances academic rigor with extracurricular enrichment, fostering lifelong learners and responsible citizens.

Academic Programs

Science

Comprehensive programs in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, emphasizing experimentation and critical thinking.

Mathematics

From foundational arithmetic to advanced calculus, our curriculum sharpens analytical and problem-solving skills.

Humanities

Explore History, Geography, and Literature to develop cultural awareness and analytical insight.

Arts & Music

Encouraging creativity through visual arts, performing arts, and music programs for all grade levels.

Departments

Science Department

Arts & Music Department

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is designed to provide a balanced education, integrating academic subjects with co-curricular and extracurricular activities.

  • STEM-focused learning for critical thinking
  • Humanities and social sciences for cultural awareness
  • Arts, sports, and clubs for holistic development
  • Life skills and personal growth programs
