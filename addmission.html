Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to be transparent and straightforward. Follow these simple steps to join our academic community.

1

Inquiry & Visit

Submit an inquiry form and schedule a campus tour to experience our facilities and meet our faculty.

2

Submit Application

Complete the online application form and submit all required documents by the deadline.

3

Assessment & Interview

Students may be invited for an assessment test and interview as part of the evaluation process.

4

Admission Decision

Receive your admission decision within 2-3 weeks after completing all requirements.

Admission Requirements

To ensure a smooth application process, please review the following requirements before submitting your application.

Application Form

Complete the online application form with accurate information. Incomplete forms may delay the admission process.

Academic Records

Submit transcripts or report cards from the previous two academic years, along with any standardized test scores.

Recommendation Letters

Two recommendation letters from teachers or school administrators who know the applicant well.

Health Records

Provide up-to-date immunization records and a recent physical examination report.

Identification Documents

Submit a copy of the applicant's birth certificate and a recent passport-sized photograph.

Application Fee

A non-refundable application fee of $75 is required to process your application.
